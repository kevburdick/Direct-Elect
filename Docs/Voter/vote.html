Vote

Instructions

Vote for a candidate by selecting the radio button next to their name. If the candidate you wish to vote for is not on the ballot, choose "Write-In" and enter your candidate's first and last name. Answer any questions on the ballot you would like by selecting the radio button "Yes" if you are in favor and "No" if you are against the proposition. Once you are finished making your selections, hit the "Submit" button to finalize your vote.
